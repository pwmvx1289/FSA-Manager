Part 5: Get Involved
Wow, you're a fast learner. Congratulations on getting through all the steps. Make sure you didn't skip anything! You are now ready to get even more involved in the FSA Program. Ready? Get set...GO!

Step 1: Join a Firefox Club
Joining a Firefox Club gives you a chance to meet new friends and work as a team. First check if there's already a Firefox Club at your school with our Firefox Club Directory. If there's not, consider starting one!

Step 2: Meet your Regional Ambassador Leads
Regional Ambassador Leads are connected to Firefox Clubs and FSAs in their country and serve as their guide. Know who they are and how to contact them. Reach out to them if you begin setting up events at your school.

Step 3: Find Your First Activity To Do
Now you are ready to do your first activity! Head over to the Activities wiki page and pick one to start or come up with one to do! Many activities lead to recognition, free swag, and opportunities to work with Mozilla staff. You will meet tons of new people and learn tons of new skills in marketing, management, and technology. The growing never stops with us.

Step 4: Congratulations!
We want to thank you for joining us and congratulate you on starting your awesome journey with Firefox. Remember, you get what you put in so go above and beyond.
